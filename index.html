About-Us

The School serves the best as Educational Institute



The school also, is affiliated to NIOS board for class X & XII and Prayag Sangeet Samiti Allahabad and has NCC wing attached to Air force Kanpur for providing NCC training to both Boys and Girls Students.


To develop, competitive spirit and all round growth of personality, the school has adopted House-System namely; Aravali, Shivalik, Nilgiri and Satpura Houses, where in all the students have been grouped house wise for indoor and outdoor competitions, like Inter-House. English and Hindi Debates, Essay-writing, Elocution, Extempore Speech, Quiz, Group discussion, Group Songs, folk dances classical Dance (Kathak), Instrumental Music as, well as art and craft competitions. Similarly for outdoor competitions, we conduct Inter-House volley Ball, Cricket, Kho-Kho, Football, Kabaddi and Athletics with track events. The Indoor, Inter-House competition namely; Chess, Carrom and Table-Tennis are regularly organized, to develop team spirit and competitive sprit amongst all students martial arts like Taekwondo and Karate as well adventure sports, to boys and girls for self- defence, are part of school curriculum.
